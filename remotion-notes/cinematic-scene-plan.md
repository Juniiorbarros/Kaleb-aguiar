# Remotion Cinematic Scene Plan - Kaleb Aguiar

## Composition 1: KalebHeroTheaterLoop

- Format: 1920x1080, 8-12 seconds, loopable.
- Use: website hero background and poster extraction.
- Visual: dark theater, chandelier glow, champagne fabric close-up, soft parallax, gold title reveal.
- Motion: slow light sweep, fabric drift, title fade/rise, minimal camera push.
- Output: WebM, MP4, WebP poster.

## Composition 2: KalebLinkpageReel

- Format: 1080x1920, 12-15 seconds.
- Use: mobile linkpage background or campaign reel.
- Visual: logo, vertical dress reveal, category beats: debutante, madrinha, formatura, prova.
- Motion: editorial cuts with 1.5-2s per beat, no frantic transitions.
- Output: MP4 and still poster.

## Composition 3: KalebDressReveal

- Format: 1080x1350 and 1080x1920 variants.
- Use: social preview and section background.
- Visual: close-ups of bordado, cintura, saia, brilho and final CTA.
- Motion: detail macro, masked text reveal, gold line drawing.

## Production rules

- Product photos must be real approved assets.
- Generated imagery can support atmosphere only.
- Always render poster fallback for mobile.
- Keep text overlays in code where possible instead of baking all copy into video.
